Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is one of the longest sea beaches in the world. It's a sandy beach with an unbroken 155 Km length. The sea beach is the main attraction of Cox's Bazar town. There is large number of hotels, resorts, guest houses, motels and restaurants have been established in the city. There are also a few very old wooden Buddhist temples at Ramu, a few kilometers from Cox's Bazar, well worth visiting.A drive to Teknaf, which is the southernmost tip of mainland of Bangladesh, is a memorable journey. A day trip to either Moheshkhali or Sonadia, the deltaic islands nestled among the gentle waves of the Bay of Bengal, will also be really interesting. Other attractions for visitors are the conch shell market, tribal handicraft, salt, and prawn cultivation.Himachal,Inani Beach, Maheskhali,Ramu and Sonadia Island these are the main tourists attraction here you can visit.
